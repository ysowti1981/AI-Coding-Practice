# House Layout

## Rooms
- **Living Room:** Main common area on the ground floor. Has a smart thermostat.
- **Bedroom:** Upstairs. Has a smart thermostat.

## Doors
- **Front Door:** Main entrance, ground floor. Equipped with a smart lock.
- **Back Door:** Rear entrance from the kitchen to the backyard. Equipped with a smart lock.

## Garage
- **Garage:** Attached single-car garage with a smart garage door opener.
- The garage connects to the house through an interior door (not smart-controlled).

## Device Locations
- Living room thermostat is on the wall by the hallway.
- Bedroom thermostat is next to the bedroom door.
- Front door lock is a deadbolt with keypad.
- Back door lock is a deadbolt with keypad.
- Garage door opener is ceiling-mounted with a wall button and smart control.

## Things to Check When Leaving
When the user leaves the house, the following should be verified:
- Front door: should be closed and locked.
- Back door: should be closed and locked.
- Garage: should be closed.
- Thermostats: can be set to energy-saving mode (18 °C).

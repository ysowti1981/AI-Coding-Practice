# Device Specifications

## Thermostats
- **Model:** SmartTemp Pro 3000
- **Supported temperature range:** 15 °C – 30 °C
- **Precision:** 0.5 °C increments
- **Modes:** Heat, Cool, Auto, Off
- **Response time:** Temperature changes take approximately 10–15 minutes to take effect.

## Smart Locks (Doors)
- **Model:** SecureLock Z-200
- **States:** Open / Closed (locked)
- **Features:** Keypad entry, remote lock/unlock, auto-lock after 5 minutes.

## Garage Door Opener
- **Model:** GarageMaster 500
- **States:** Open / Closed
- **Features:** Remote open/close, auto-close after 10 minutes if left open.
- **Safety:** Obstacle detection sensor prevents closing if something is in the way.
